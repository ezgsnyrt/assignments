Cream of Mushroom Soup Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Nutrition Facts
  • Directions
  • Suggestions

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of sliced fresh mushrooms Mushroom
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1,5 liter of water
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered black pepper
  • 1 glass of milk
  • a pinch of fresh dill

Nutrition Facts

One bowl of the soup has 165 calories including;

  • 11 grams of fat
  • 14 grams of carbs
  • 5 grams of protein

Directions

Here are the steps to be followed;

  1. Cut the mushrooms, which you have cleaned with a damp cloth, into small pieces or thin slices. Dice the dry onion into small cubes.
  2. Put olive oil and butter in a pot, and sauté the onion.
  3. Add the mushrooms and sauté until they release their liquid.
  4. Add the flour and sauté for 2 minutes
  5. Add the water and cook by stirring the mixture until it begins to boil over medium heat.
  6. After it boils, stir the mixture and add the milk.
  7. Before removing from the heat, add salt, black pepper, and dill. Bring it to a boil once more.
  8. Your soup is ready to serve. Enjoy your meal!

Suggestions

It may be good to consider the following suggestions. You can; ;

  • Prefer any type of milk while making the soup. Oat milk was used in this recipe.
  • Use parsley instead of dill considering your taste preferences.
